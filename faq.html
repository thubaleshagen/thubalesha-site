Frequently Asked Questions

thubalesha's Fragrance

How do I place an order?

Browse our products, select the fragrance you like and click the Buy Now or Add to Cart button. Follow the checkout process and complete your payment.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept secure payments through debit cards, credit cards, Instant EFT, and Buy Now Pay Later services such as Payflex and PayJustNow.

How does Buy Now Pay Later work?

Buy Now Pay Later allows customers to receive their product immediately and pay for it in smaller instalments over time. Approval for this payment option is handled directly by the payment provider.

How long does delivery take?

Orders are processed within 1–3 business days and delivery normally takes 2–5 business days depending on your location in South Africa.

Can I return a product?

Yes. Unused products in their original packaging can be returned within 7 days of receiving the order.
